Advanced Concepts:

Big O Notation - How quickly your application will increase in time or use up resources.  Think of a car that accelerates quickly, too fast and you can speed out of control.
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Big_O_notation 

Complexity - Describes the interactions between all the entities in the program.  The more complex your program is the harder it is to change it without affecting all the other parts.
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Programming_complexity
